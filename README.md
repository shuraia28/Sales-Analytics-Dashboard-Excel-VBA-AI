# Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-Excel-VBA-AI

This **Sales Analytics Dashboard** was developed using **Microsoft Excel, VBA Automation, and AI-assisted techniques** to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer behavior, profitability, and service-wise revenue distribution. The dashboard enables business leaders and sales managers to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations and dynamic filtering capabilities.

## Dashboard Objective

The primary purpose of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor overall sales performance
* Track profitability and margin trends
* Analyze customer acquisition and retention
* Evaluate sales performance by service category
* Compare new and repeat customer sales
* Identify top-performing customers
* Support strategic business decisions through interactive reporting
